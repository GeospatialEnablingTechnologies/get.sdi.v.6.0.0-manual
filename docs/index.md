# GET SDI Portal

**GET SDI Portal**, is a web mapping application providing a comprehensive **platform** for viewing,
downloading, analyzing, querying, editing and styling data originating from multiple geospatial
sources. It constitutes a simple and **ready to deploy solution** for any organization who wants to
setup a Geoportal based on ISO/OGC Standards.  

## What you can do
- **Data visualization** in 2D and interactive 3D (globe with terrain relief)
- **Data query & selection** based on attribute and spatial criteria, with a quick feature-search bar
- **Data download** in multiple formats (GeoJSON, Shapefile, GML, KML, GPX, GeoTIFF)
- **Editing & updating** of geospatial features through **WFS-T** transactions (draw, modify, delete, edit attributes via forms)
- **Metadata catalogue search** (CSW) against internal and external catalogues, with INSPIRE-compliant search criteria
- **Layer management** with a drag-and-drop tree, grouping, visibility and transparency control, per-layer actions and dynamic **CQL** filtering of live layers
- **Add data** from a wide range of sources: WMS, WFS, WMTS and CSW services; vector files (GeoJSON, KML, GPX, DXF, Shapefile); and cloud-optimized imagery via **STAC** catalogues
- **Basemap switching** and advanced **CRS handling**, including coordinate entry in multiple reference systems
- **Navigation & measurement** tools (distance, area, circle and angle)
- **Built-in styling** through an integrated **SLD** style editor (point/line/polygon symbols, labels and rule-based styling), with selectable alternative symbologies per layer
- **Feature identification** with configurable **right-click interactions** and smart formatting (currency, percentages, areas, dates, links, images)
- **Geocoding** (address search and reverse geocoding)
- **Layer comparison** (swipe), map **bookmarks** and **temporal** dimension support
- **Print & export**: PDF output with paper size, orientation, scale, dynamic title/comment and optional legend, plus map screenshot export
- **Interactive dashboards**: analytics dashboards (counters, bar and pie charts, data tables, embedded maps, rich text) and map dashboards with spatial cross-filtering
- **Document & media library** with hierarchical folders and file preview/download

## Identity

- **Ready to Deploy**
- **ISO/OGC compliant**
    - WMS, WMTS
    - WFS / WFS-T
    - WPS
    - CSW
    - KML
- **2D & 3D** mapping engine
- **Central configuration** database (maps, dashboards, layers, settings)
